Awesome Baked Crispy Chicken Wings



Ingredients










Baked Cream Cheese Wontons



Ingredients





Instructions


Step One
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Line a shallow baking dish with aluminum foil and grease lightly.

Step Two
Cut tips from chicken wings and discard.
Cut between the joint to separate each wing into 2 pieces.
Step Three
Combine butter and garlic in a small saucepan over low heat;
heat until butter is melted and garlic is fragrant, 3 to 5 minutes.
Step Four
Mix Parmesan cheese, parsley, oregano, paprika, salt,
and pepper together in a shallow bowl.
Step Five
Dip chicken wing pieces in melted butter mixture; coat with
Parmesan cheese mixture in the bowl. Arrange in the prepared baking dish.
Drizzle any remaining butter on top.
Step Six
Bake in the preheated oven until skin is browned and juices run clear,
about 1 hour.

Instructions


Step One
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Spray a baking sheet with cooking spray.

Step Two
Lay wonton wrappers on prepared baking sheet.
Place 1 teaspoon cream cheese in the center of each
wonton wrapper.
Step Three
Lightly brush edges of wonton wrappers with water
and fold each wrapper in half to form a triangle.
Press edges to seal. Fold the tips of each triangle up
together so that they meet in above the center of the
wonton and press tips together to seal. Spray assembled
wontons with cooking spray.
Step Four
Bake in the preheated oven until wontons are crispy
and golden and the cream cheese is melted, 5 to 7 minutes.

Instructions


Step One
Combine popcorn and 1/4 cup olive oil in a
2-quart saucepan; cover. Cook over high heat,
continuously shaking the pan, until the popcorn is
popped and the lid begins to lift from the popcorn,
about 5 minutes. Pour popcorn into a large bowl.

Step Two
Mix 2 tablespoons olive oil, Italian seasoning,
and garlic salt into popcorn; toss to coat.
Add Parmesan cheese and mix well.

Instructions


Step One
Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
Add beef and Italian sausage; cook and stir
until browned, about 8 minutes.
Drain excess grease. Let cool slightly.

Step Two
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water
to a boil. Cook manicotti shells in the boiling
water, stirring occasionally until tender yet
firm to the bite, about 7 minutes. Drain.
Step Three
Mix cooled beef and sausage, cottage cheese,
1 jar pizza sauce, and mozzarella cheese together
in a large bowl. Stuff shells generously with mixture.
Step Four
Arrange stuffed shells on a baking sheet and freeze
until firm, 15 to 20 minutes. Cut each shell into
2 or 3 lasagna pieces.
Step Five
Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan
to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Step Six
Place flour in a bowl. Beat eggs
in another bowl. Pour bread crumbs onto
a shallow plate.
Step Seven
Dredge stuffed lasagna pieces in
flour. Dip in beaten eggs. Roll in bread
crumbs until coated.
Step Eight
Fry 4 breaded lasagna pieces at a
time in the hot oil until golden brown,
3 to 5 minutes. Repeat with remaining pieces.
Step Nine
Pour remaining jar of pizza sauce
into a bowl for dipping.
