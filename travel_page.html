Travel to Japan!

Japan, an Island in The Pacific

Japan is one of the biggest islands in the Pacific Ocean. With over one hundred million people, Japan is a very busy place. The capital, Tokyo, has many sites to see, including historic buildings and the Tokyo Skytree! Japan is also the home to anime and manga, which are japanese based movies, shows and comics!

Cherry Blossom Tress

Japan is an amazing country to go travel to, escepially in the spring.
This is when the cherry blossom tress blossom, and is an amazing site to see!
You might the cherry blossom tree festivals too!
