A couple of days ago, I got my server that I bought

3/01/19

Today I have been watching Hermitcraft. For you guys that don't
know what that is, it's basically a team of Minecrafting
youtubers who all play on one server. It's pretty fun to watch,
as you get to everyone's perspective of the world. I also get
a lot of ideas from watching to use when I am playing and for my
small Minecraft Youtube channel. I always enjoy watching them
in my free time! One of my favorite Hermitcrafters is Iskall. He is an awesome Youtuber and I always love to watch his channels.
